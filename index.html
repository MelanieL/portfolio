ABOUT

A little bit about me...

I began professional life younger than most thanks to my bilingual (English/French) skills. I spent 12 years working my way up at a manufacturing & distribution company. But in the last few years, an itch to pursue coding led me to study it in my spare time.

Self-discipline allowed me to develop the skills needed to get accepted into HackerYou's Web Development Bootcamp, an intense 9 week program. In late 2017, I left my career to pursue this new path.

The pace of the tech industry is a natural fit with my constant desire to learn and grow. I'm passionate about building everything from basic websites to IoT devices.
