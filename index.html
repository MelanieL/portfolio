ABOUT

I'm a Bilingual Web Developer with a desire to learn, drink coffee and be outdoors.

I started my professional life at a young age because of my ability to communicate in English and French. I spent 12 years with that company, working my way up and learning all I could about every aspect of business. But in the last few years, an itch to pursue my passion for code led me to study it in my spare time.

A combination of self-discipline and passion allowed me to develop the skills needed to get accepted into the full-time HackerYou bootcamp, an intense 9 week Web Development program. In late 2017, I decided to leave my career (on good terms) to pursue this new path.

The tech world is changing rapidly. When I started my career 12 years ago, Blackberries were just becoming a “thing”. Now, technology is everywhere. The pace of the tech industry feels like a natural fit with my constant desire to learn and grow.

I love playing with everything from basic websites to IoT devices. Anyone I’ve worked with would say that I have common sense, I’m a problem solver and that I’m precise in my work.

When I’m not immersing myself in code, I love reading, gaming, rock climbing and anything outdoors. I have two dogs, Nigel (Golden Retriever) and Piper (Border Collie).
